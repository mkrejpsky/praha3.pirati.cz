Expats

You can vote in the municipal assembly election!

…however you have to fulfill these criteria:

  • Be citizen of the European Union.
  • Be at least 18 years old on the second day of the election.
  • Have residence in the Czech Republic as on the election day (has a permanent residence permit or certificate of temporary residence).
  • Have a residence in the municipality in which you want to vote.

…do you meet all the criteria? Make these steps!

Apply for registration in the voter list at the municipal office in your place of residence:

In person

  • Fill out application
  • Bring your ID or passport.
  • Bring your certificate of permanent or temporary residence.
  • Go to “Organizační odbor”, Lipanská 7: praha3.cz/urad/organizacni-struktura/odbor-organizacni

Post or electronically

  • Fill out application
  • State at least your name, surname and date of birth, for further identification the address of residence in the Czech Republic. Other data can be verified by the municipal authority in the register of foreigners.
  • Send it by post or electronically using data box.

…and don´t miss the deadline!

You can apply to be entered at any time, but at the latest 2 days before Election Day (i.e. on the Wednesday before the election up to 16.00).

More details can be found here: https://www.mvcr.cz/volby/clanek/for-foreigners-voters.aspx
